About 1 Link Me

One link to share them all

In a world where we constantly share content—websites, social profiles, articles, tools, and resources— sending multiple links can quickly become messy and overwhelming. 1 Link Me is a simple, free online link-sharing service that solves this problem by turning up to 8 links into one clean, shareable URL.

What Is 1 Link Me?

1 Link Me is a lightweight, no-login tool that lets you combine multiple links into a single, easy-to-share link. Instead of sending a long list of URLs, you can now share just one link—perfect for messaging, social media, emails, or anywhere simplicity matters.

Paste your links → Click convert → Share one link. No sign-up. No cost. No complications.

Why We Created 1 Link Me

Sharing multiple links shouldn’t be complicated. Whether you're:

  • a creator sharing your content
  • a business sending resources
  • a teacher organizing materials
  • or just someone sending useful links to friends

You deserve a fast, clean, and effortless way to share everything at once. 1 Link Me was built to eliminate clutter and save time.

How It Works

Unlike traditional link tools, 1 Link Me works without storing your data on a server.

  • Your links are encoded directly into the shareable URL
  • When someone opens it, the page reads the data and displays your links instantly
  • No backend. No database.

This makes the tool fast, private, and simple.

Privacy First Approach

We believe in keeping things simple—and private.

  • We do not store your links on any server
  • There are no accounts
  • Your data exists only inside your generated link

Important: Because links are not stored, lost links cannot be recovered. Make sure to save your generated URL.
